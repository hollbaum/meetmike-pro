## Project Overview

MAIC/MAX is an AI consulting service that helps businesses solve digital problems. The client experience is conversational - they don't choose specific products, they just describe their problem and MAX handles the rest.

## User Journey Phases (The MAX Pipeline)

| Phase | Agent | Purpose |
|-------|-------|---------|
| 1 | **Lead Generator** | "Pulse" engine crawls global market for trends, finds clients with digital problems, drives traffic to meetmike.pro |
| 2 | **First Responder** | Welcomes client, mirrors their language ("talks after the client's mouth"), makes them feel understood |
| 3 | **Project Manager** | Gets problem description, scope, deadline. Always human-in-the-loop. Determines what email to send |
| 4 | **Orchestra** | Creates solution mockup/visualization - NOT the final product, just a preview |
| 5 | **Closer** | Negotiates agreement: price, quality, scope, timeline, deposit. Uses value-based pricing (client business impact, not hours). Gets contract signed |
| 6 | **Project Manager** (Delivery) | Takes over after contract. Ensures Orchestra delivers on time, quality, and budget |

## Post-Contract Workflow

1. **Digital contract signing** - Button click / picture verification
2. **Stripe payment** - Deposit collected before work begins
3. **Client onboarding**:
   - Create user account for client
   - Assign to dedicated workspace
   - Create dedicated Teams channel for that project
4. **Iterative delivery** - Orchestra + PM agents interact with client throughout
5. **Human-in-the-loop** - Multiple client interactions required for quality delivery

**IMPORTANT**: Do NOT believe you can solve a case or deliver a product without MULTIPLE interactions with the client. This is collaborative, not fire-and-forget.

## Key Concepts

### Value-Based Pricing
Price is determined by how much value the solution creates for the client's business - NOT by how long it takes to build.

### Teaser Format
Email proposals show the beginning of the solution clearly, but blur/hide the rest. This creates engagement - client must interact to unlock full proposal.

### Show of Force
Part of the mockup includes a full workspace view - client can see all the documents and structure laid out, but cannot enter or edit them. Demonstrates capability and builds trust.

### Digital Coworkers (Eidolons)
The AI agents that do the work. From Greek "eidolon" meaning spirit-image or phantom. Clients don't need to understand the tech - they just get results.
